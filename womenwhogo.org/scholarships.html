GopherCon Scholarhip



Eligibility

You must meet at least one of the following requirements:

  • Identify as a woman or non-binary
  • Identify as a person of color
  • Identify as LGBTQIA+
  • Identify as disabled

What's Covered

Our goal is to send as many people as possible to GopherCon. Each scholarship is different, depending on what you need help with:

  • GopherCon ticket
  • Plane tickets
  • Hotel/Airbnb
  • Food, taxi, etc.

If your employer can pay for any part of your expenses, we ask that you take advantage of that first and use the scholarship for only uncovered expenses.
